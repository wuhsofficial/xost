LearnHub

The Challenge

As LearnHub expanded its user base globally, their AWS CloudFront bandwidth bills grew exponentially. High video streaming costs were eroding their profit margins, while students in remote regions still suffered from buffering and poor resolution playback.

The XOST Solution

We engineered a highly sophisticated Multi-CDN (Content Delivery Network) routing strategy. Instead of relying on a single provider, our dynamic routing engine evaluates real-time bandwidth costs and geographic latency across Cloudflare, Fastly, and AWS. Video requests are automatically served from the fastest and most cost-effective edge node available at that exact millisecond.

The Impact

The intelligent edge routing slashed data transfer out (DTO) costs immediately without compromising quality. In fact, video playback quality improved worldwide, supporting seamless 4K educational streams.
