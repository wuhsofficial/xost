The Challenge

MedixHealth, a vast network of clinical facilities, was operating on aging on-premise servers. The infrastructure was increasingly difficult to maintain, prone to hardware failures, and crucially, was failing to meet modern HIPAA compliance standards for electronic health records (EHR). The risk of a massive data breach was looming.

The XOST Solution

We designed a secure, HIPAA-compliant cloud environment on Azure. The cornerstone of this migration was a strict Zero-Trust Network Architecture (ZTNA). We implemented identity-aware proxies, strict micro-segmentation of clinical data from administrative networks, and end-to-end AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit.

The Impact

The seamless cloud transition modernized MedixHealth's entire operational capability. Doctors now access patient records instantly and securely from any facility, while IT administration overhead has plummeted.
