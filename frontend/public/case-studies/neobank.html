The Challenge

Digital-first NeoBank was losing 40% of prospective customers during account creation. Their Know Your Customer (KYC) onboarding process required human compliance officers to manually review uploaded ID documents, leading to a frustrating 48-hour approval delay.

The XOST Solution

We built a hyper-efficient automated KYC pipeline utilizing Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and Optical Character Recognition (OCR). When a user uploads an ID, the OCR extracts data instantly, verifies it against government databases via API, and uses computer vision to detect forged documents. The RPA bot handles the entire backend approval workflow.

The Impact

The transformation turned onboarding into NeoBank's strongest competitive advantage. Human officers now only review the 5% of edge cases flagged by the AI, while 95% of users are approved automatically, day or night.
