The Challenge

RouteOptima managed a fleet of over 500 delivery vehicles across complex metropolitan areas. However, their dispatchers were spending up to 6 hours a day manually planning routes using outdated software. This resulted in inefficient fuel consumption, missed delivery windows, and exhausted drivers.

The XOST Solution

We developed a custom AI predictive routing algorithm powered by real-time data ingestion. The system evaluates live traffic data, historical delivery times, weather conditions, and vehicle capacity. Built on Python and TensorFlow, the AI dynamically recalculates and dispatches the most mathematically optimal routes instantly.

The Impact

The difference was immediate. Dispatchers transitioned from manual planners to strategic supervisors. The AI algorithm consistently discovered routing efficiencies humans couldn't, vastly reducing operational overhead and carbon footprint.
