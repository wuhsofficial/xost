ShopStream

E-Commerce

The Challenge

ShopStream, an emerging fast-fashion giant, faced a recurring nightmare: catastrophic site crashes during flash sales and Black Friday events. Their rigid, provisioned infrastructure simply couldn't scale fast enough to accommodate sudden 10x traffic spikes, resulting in frustrated customers and devastating revenue losses.

The XOST Solution

We completely re-architected ShopStream's backend using a Serverless methodology. By utilizing AWS Lambda for dynamic compute, DynamoDB for infinitely scalable NoSQL storage, and API Gateway for request throttling, we decoupled the monolithic codebase into highly resilient serverless functions that scale horizontally in milliseconds.

The Impact

During the very next holiday season, ShopStream experienced its highest traffic surge in history—with absolutely zero downtime. Better yet, because they only pay for exact compute time used, their baseline infrastructure costs dropped significantly during non-peak hours.
